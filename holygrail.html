Holy Grail

My Holy Grail is the Toltoys Darth Vader 12-Back, Mint on Card (MOC).

I was very fortunate to obtain this figure as these figures are extremely rare to find and are the envy of all collectors. A lot of collectors from around the world wish to have a 12 Back Toltoys; especially still attached to its original card.

The Story behind what was thought to be worthless toys; sold for $52,350

There were 6 out of a 11 of these rare figures up for auction on the TradeMe website in February 2022. To have six of these figures up for auction at the same time is very rare; if not non-existing at all.

The story behind to how these figures appeared on TradeMe is extraordinary in itself. The owner of the figures, which was the mother, had just recently passed. A friend from way down South flew up North to help her friend clear and pack her mothers deceased things.

As the daughter was going through her mothers things; she came across these 6 Star Wars figurines. Thinking they were worth nothing; she threw them into the rubbish. Her friend, who noticed this happen, asked if she could take the figurines back down South to give to her grandson. When she approached her grandson to give him the figures; he was not interested as they were not Fortnite.

She was fortunate enough that another friend suggested that they may be worth something and she should put them on TradeMe. Therefore, she posted them on TradeMe with no idea how much they were worth. She posted them at a starting bid of $25 each. She was originally going to put them up for a $35 Buy Now.

The six figures went for a total worth of $52,350.

Why are this Holy Grails so rare?

The main variant is the Toltoys logo on the front of the card; contrast to the more common one Kenner. Image

Toltoys Image Kenner (Caption)

After the Early Bird set; the first 12 figures were released by Kenner on a 12 Back A Card. The first 12 were:

Han Solo

Chewbacca

Princess Leia Organa

Luke Skywalker

See-Threepio (C3P0)

Artoo-Detoo (R2D2)

Darth Vader

Ben (Obi-Wan) Kenobi

Stormtrooper

Death Squad Commander

Sand People

Jawa (image 12 Back A set)

When Kenner produced their 3rd wave of figures called the 12 Back C (which has very slight difference of text on the back of the card between the three waves) they mailed loose figures to Hong Kong and Taiwan so as to save on shipping costs.

The figures were then sent to Australia and were packaged in Australia under the Toltoys logo. Even though they were Kenner (USA) 12 Back C figures, the ones packaged in Australia are considered to be their 12 Back A Card.

The sale of the Toltoys logo figures was exclusive to Australia and New Zealand only. Since there was only a limited amount of figures distributed to Australasia, there were only a limited amount of Toltoys figures packaged. Furthermore, Kiwi and Australian kids were very eager to rip the figure off its card so to play with it. Therefore, to find any MOC Toltoys is extremely rare.
